Driver_fan_5V

This design allows the piloting of a fan powered at 5V by means of a control signal from 3.3V .

In my case , I had available the GPIO of the pi- Raspberry and I wanted the fan turning on and went out automatically and intelligently based on the temperature detected by the CPU .

Here you can find the schematic of the circuit that I made , and scripts that I made for the piloting of a GPIO pins via two board : Raspberry-pi and Orange-pi
